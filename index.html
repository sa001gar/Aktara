OUR HERITAGE

39 Years of
Timeless Craftsmanship

Founded in 1985 by master weaver Smt. Kamala Devi, Aktara began as a humble workshop in the heart of Varanasi. What started with a simple dream to preserve India's rich textile heritage has blossomed into a globally recognized brand.

Our journey is woven with threads of tradition, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to quality. Each saree tells a story - of skilled artisans who pour their heart into every thread, of ancient techniques passed down through generations.

Today, we work with over 200 master artisans across India, ensuring fair wages and preserving traditional crafts for future generations.
